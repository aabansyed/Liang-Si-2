ALMONDS

AFGHANI MAMRA

Afghani Mamra almonds are prized for their rich oil content and crunchy texture. Perfect for snacking and traditional recipes.

 CALIFORNIA ALMONDS

California almonds are widely popular for their size and mild flavor. Ideal for roasting or making almond milk.

 SPANISH MARCONA

Marcona almonds from Spain are sweeter and softer. Often used in gourmet cuisine and desserts.

 ROASTED ALMONDS

Roasted almonds offer a nutty, deep flavor and are a perfect healthy snack for any time of day.

 ALMOND SLICES

Almond slices are great for baking, toppings, or adding crunch to salads and cereals.
